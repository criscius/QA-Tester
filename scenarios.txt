BDD: Behavior-Driven Development

Given: I am on the login page
When: I type an invalid email.
Then: It should show an error message saying "Incorrect email".

// ============================================================= 
// ==========# Test Scenarios: Password Recovery ===============
// =============================================================

## Scenario 1: Successful Password Reset (Happy Path)
**Given** I am on the "Forgot Password" page
**When** I enter a valid registered email address
**And** I click on the "Reset Password" button
**Then** I should see a success message: "A reset link has been sent to your email."

## Scenario 2: Empty Email Field (Edge Case)
**Given** I am on the "Forgot Password" page
**When** I leave the email field empty
**And** I click on the "Reset Password" button
**Then** I should see a validation error: "Email is required."
